Meet Rhina

Hi, I'm Rhina, founder of Naples Professional Home Organizer — a faith-centered luxury organization service for Naples' most discerning residents.

My mission is simple: create serene, functional spaces that enhance your lifestyle and reflect biblical order. As 1 Corinthians 14:40 reminds us, "Let all things be done decently and in order." This verse is the heartbeat of every closet, bedroom, and home I transform.

With over a decade of experience in high-end residential organization, I specialize in walk-in closets, luxury wardrobes, and master suites. Discretion, precision, and excellence define my work.

Whether you're transitioning seasons, downsizing, or craving daily peace in your space, I bring calm, clarity, and beauty to your home — one perfectly folded sweater at a time.
